Bamboo


Bamboo is something that has been in the game for a few updates. However, in the 1.20 update it will become a wood type! This means you'll be able to make stairs, slabs, doors, trapdoors, rafts, etc. from it like you could with other types, such as oak, birch, and mangrove.
